Facts about Bushfires 2019

The Australian bushfire has led to a massive loss to wildlife, forest area and has even caused human casualties, including firefighters from the U.S. It has even affected the air quality in nearby cities such as Sydney and Melbourne.

  • Nearly 500 million animals lost
  • Age old forests perished
  • Wildfires triggering thunderstorms
